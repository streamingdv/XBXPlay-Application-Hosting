# XBXPlay Application Hosting

Welcome to the XBXPlay application hosting repository. XBXPlay is a third-party Xbox Remote Play client for Android, optimized for low-latency game streaming on phones, tablets, Android TV devices, handhelds, and more.

## Download

Download the current standalone APK from the [Releases page](https://github.com/streamingdv/XBXPlay-Application-Hosting/releases/).

XBXPlay is currently available for Android devices running Android 6.0 or newer.

## Key Features

- **1080p Remote Play**: Stream Xbox Series X|S and Xbox One games at up to 1080p.
- **FSR Video Upscaling**: Enhance image clarity with AMD FidelityFX Super Resolution video upscaling.
- **Low-Latency Streaming**: Optimized streaming pipeline for responsive Remote Play.
- **Manual Bitrate Adjustment**: Tune the stream bitrate to fit your network connection.
- **Xbox 360 Backward-Compatible Games**: Play supported Xbox 360 games through your console.
- **Custom Onscreen Controls**: Create touch gamepad layouts tailored to individual games.
- **Controller Support**: Use USB-OTG and Bluetooth controllers with customizable button mapping.
- **Virtual Xbox Gamepad**: Use your Android device as a virtual gamepad for your Xbox.
- **Fullscreen Streaming**: Play without black bars using fullscreen scaling options.
- **Picture-in-Picture and Multi-Window**: Continue watching or playing while using other apps on supported Android versions.
- **External Display Support**: Enjoy Remote Play on a connected external display or with Dual-Screen support on devices like the Ayn Thor.
- **Performance Overlay**: Monitor important streaming performance metrics while playing.
- **Experimental Local Multiplayer**: Support for local multiplayer scenarios.
- **Android TV and Handheld Support**: Designed for Android TV devices, Odin, Retroid Pocket, Ayn Thor, Amazon Fire TV, Meta Quest, phones, and tablets.

## Android Version

Download XBXPlay from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.grill.xbxplay), or download the standalone APK from the latest [GitHub release](https://github.com/streamingdv/XBXPlay-Application-Hosting/releases/).

The standalone APK has no Google Play dependencies, making it suitable for devices without the Play Store, including Amazon Fire TV, Meta Quest, and certain Android handhelds.

For the best streaming experience, use a wired network connection for your Xbox where possible, connect your Android device to 5 GHz Wi-Fi, and use a stable internet connection when playing remotely.
